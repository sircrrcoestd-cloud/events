24‑Hour Hackathon

CRR CodeForge

Non‑stop 24‑hour product build where teams design, code and pitch a working prototype around real‑world problem statements.

Team size: 2–4 • Reg. fee: ₹300 per team • Domains: Web, App, AI/ML.

How to register – CRR CodeForge

  1. Create a team of 2–4 members from any year/branch.
  2. Decide a team name and choose your preferred domain (Web/App/AI).
  3. Open the Hackathon Google Form link given in the Register button.
  4. Fill in team details, college IDs, email and phone numbers carefully.
  5. Complete payment at the fest registration desk or via UPI as mentioned in the form.
  6. Upload payment screenshot in the form and submit.
  7. Wait for confirmation mail/WhatsApp message from the coordinators.
Robotics Arena

Robo Rumble & Line Tracer

Bring your wired or wireless bots for intense arena battles and precision line‑following challenges.

Team size: 2–3 • Categories: Robo War, Line Follower • Limited slots.

How to register – Robotics Events

  1. Form a team of 2–3 members and select event type (Robo War / Line Follower / Both).
  2. Check bot dimensions, weight limits and voltage rules from the rulebook PDF shared on the form.
  3. Open the Robotics Registration Form and enter team + bot details.
  4. Pay the event fee as per category and keep the UPI transaction ID ready.
  5. Upload the payment proof and submit the form.
  6. Join the WhatsApp group link shown after form submission for updates.
E‑Sports

CRR Gaming Arena

Competitive gaming showdowns with titles like BGMI, Valorant and FC, complete with live casting and leaderboards.

Solo & Squad modes • On‑spot finals • Exciting prize pool.

How to register – Gaming Arena

  1. Choose your game (BGMI / Valorant / FC) and mode (Solo / Squad).
  2. Keep your in‑game ID, team name and captain contact number ready.
  3. Fill the Gaming Registration Form and select preferred slot.
  4. Pay the entry fee online or at the gaming desk.
  5. Join the event Discord / WhatsApp server for lobby codes & rules.
Talks & Workshops

AI, Cloud & Startups

Deep‑dive sessions from industry experts on AI, cloud, cybersecurity, and how to turn your side‑project into a startup.

Open to all branches • Limited seats • E‑certificates provided.

How to register – AI & Tech Talks

  1. Select the sessions you want (AI, Cloud, Cybersecurity, Startup 101).
  2. Open the Talks Registration Form from the button provided.
  3. Enter your name, branch, year, and active email ID.
  4. Choose time slots and confirm seat availability.
  5. Submit the form; you will receive your entry QR / pass by email.

General Registration Flow

Keep this common format for any new event you add.

  1. Select the event card and read its specific rules.
  2. Click on the “Register (Google Form)” button for that event.
  3. Fill in all mandatory details (name, roll no, branch, year, contact).
  4. Complete fee payment using the UPI / cash method mentioned.
  5. Upload payment proof if asked and submit the form.
  6. Save confirmation mail / screenshot and join the official WhatsApp group.
  7. Report at the venue 30 minutes before your event start time with college ID.
